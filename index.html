About Us

AGRICULTURE MARKET

Indra Agrotech Pvt. Ltd. established in 2023 is agricultural equipment manufacturing company based in Karad, Satara. It specializes in manufacturing harvestors and transplanters with its Sapling Transplanter model AIRAVAT -12 being launched in the April 2024.
